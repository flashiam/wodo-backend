<p>Whenever we have to do the required work, and we hire a worker who will come to our house and going to help us. In this kind of situation, we should follow some <a rel="noreferrer noopener" href="https://www.thebalancecareers.com/dealing-with-everyday-people-at-work-1919405" target="_blank">guidelines</a> called customer's best practices, that will help us and the work to finish the work nicely.</p>
<p><strong>Do’s</strong></p>
<ul><li><strong>As a Customers, you are very honorable to us, you also should show respect towards workers and show basic etiquette and manners, because they are human too and also needs appreciation.&nbsp;</strong></li><li><strong>Customers must keep their personal details confidential and should not give or show to the worker.&nbsp;Otherwise, you will be responsible for this.</strong></li><li><strong>Customers should decide the work timings with respect to the schedule suitable for the worker/s.&nbsp;</strong></li><li><strong>You</strong> <strong>should review workers after the completion of work as it will help other customers later on.&nbsp;</strong></li><li><strong>Customers should pay the whole fees after the completion of work. They can also pay partly before and partly after the completion.&nbsp;</strong></li><li><strong>Customers should follow the decided schedule.&nbsp;</strong></li><li><strong>should take into consideration the health and safety of workers.&nbsp;</strong></li><li><strong>Customers negotiations should be affordable to the worker.&nbsp;</strong></li></ul>

<p><strong>Don’ts</strong></p>
<ul><li><strong>Customers should not make things complicated for workers. They should keep the work procedure simplistic.</strong></li><li><strong>Customers must not pay workers in full before completion of work.</strong></li><li><strong>As a Customers, you should not panic regarding work and should not act rashly or violently with workers. </strong></li><li><strong>Customers should not avoid clearing doubts of workers.</strong></li><li><strong>Customers should not force workers beyond their physical, or mental limits.</strong></li><li><strong>You should not leave the worker alone.</strong></li><li><strong>When a worker comes to your house, please try to be good with him/her.
